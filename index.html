FAQ's

How does BITroom’s design service work?
BITroom’s interactive pre-design feature is one of the easiest ways to choose your shop design and know the cost in advance. Besides, if you would like to renovate or customize your shop, you could book consultation and meet our designers remotely anytime. We offer one-stop service from choosing design, work with our experts, and recommended construction service.
Can BITroom work within budget?
Budget will not be a constraint for us to deliver the best design solutions for your shop. BITroom doesn’t set a minimum budget and is happy to work within your budget.
Does it cost a consultation fee?
No, it is free of charge for consultation. You can feel free to book appointments with our designers anytime, we will connect you with our experts through online meetings.
